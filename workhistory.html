My story

As well as working in the field, I am also studying towards a bachelor's degree at Haaga-Helia university. I started my career in IT during vocational school at the electronics store chain Gigantti. I was tasked with running a helpdesk for customers buying a PC. I also helped with selling various electronics such as phones, monitors and televisions. I gained a lot of meaningful experience in customer service during my time at Gigantti. My interest in computers also rose from this experience.
After Gigantti I worked at Ilmarinen, CGI and the government of Finland. Most of these jobs were similar in that they were all customer service and ICT specialist type of helpdesk jobs. I have gotten a lot of local support experience and people skills are as honed as my IT skills.
